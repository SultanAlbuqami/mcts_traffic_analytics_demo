# Ministerial Committee Alignment

This note maps the project outputs to the stated role of the Ministerial Committee of Traffic Safety.

## Committee Responsibility -> Project Evidence

1. Develop national strategies and initiatives for traffic safety
   - Evidence: `out/executive_report.md`, `out/scenario_report.md`, `out/scenario_summary.csv`
   - Value: baseline risk posture + ranked intervention packages.

2. Collect and analyze relevant data
   - Evidence: pipeline modules in `src/traffic_analytics_demo/` and outputs under `data/` + `out/`
   - Value: reproducible analytics lifecycle from raw data to decision artifacts.

3. Monitor performance of concerned entities and avoid project/regulatory conflicts
   - Evidence: common KPI layer, traceability fields, quality gate reports, lineage summary.
   - Value: shared data definitions and auditable transformations across multi-entity contexts.

4. Promote safe-driving culture via communication channels
   - Evidence: Streamlit dashboard + stakeholder pack with plain-language insights.
   - Value: executive + operational narratives that can be reused in awareness channels.

5. Submit periodic reports to the Council of Economic and Development Affairs (CEDA)
   - Evidence: `out/stakeholder_pack.md`, `out/run_summary.json`, `out/pipeline_manifest.json`
   - Value: periodic, standardized, decision-ready reporting package.

## Suggested Operating Cadence (Demo-to-Real Transition)

1. Weekly: KPI trend refresh + hotspot review.
2. Monthly: scenario re-ranking and intervention effectiveness review.
3. Quarterly: governance review (quality gate trends, lineage coverage, policy updates).

## Boundaries

1. This project is technical analytics enablement, not legal authority.
2. Final institutional reporting formats and approvals should follow official committee governance procedures.
